Make a Home Insurance claim

Need to make a new claim or get support with an existing one? We're here to help if something happens to your home.

Start a new claim

Use our AI Claims Assistant to start your claim immediately. It's available 24/7 and will guide you through every step of the process.

What you'll need

Your policy number, details of what happened, when it happened, and any photos or evidence of damage. Don't worry if you're missing something — you can add it later.

Track an existing claim

Already submitted a claim? Ask the assistant for an update, or sign in to your account to view the full status and any actions needed from you.
